OUR FOOD AND SAFETY POLICY

BAKERS-INN is committed to providing high quality, healthy product options.

We will liaise with the relevant authorities and technical experts to achieve this by:

  1. Implementing and maintaining a Food Safety Management System
  2. Understanding customer needs, incorporating them in our production processes
  3. Providing technical advice on best practices to our customers and stakeholders
    to ensure safe handling and use of our products.
  4. Providing appropriate resources and support to staff
  5. Utilising acceptable design and processing technology that assures the safety of
    the products we produce.
